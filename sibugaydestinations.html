Buluan Island Details
Full Description

Buluan Island is a picturesque white-sand island located off the coast of Ipil. It features crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and peaceful surroundings perfect for marine adventures and relaxation.

Location

Ipil, Zamboanga Sibugay

Activities
  • Swimming
  • Snorkeling
  • Kayaking
  • Beach activities
  • Marine life observation
Best Time to Visit

November to May (dry season)

Entrance Fee

Free

Opening Hours

24/7

Travel Tips
  • Bring sunscreen and drinking water.
  • Use snorkeling gear for reef exploration.
  • Travel early for calmer waters.
  • Keep the island clean and avoid damaging corals.
